Symptoms

Fever

If you have temperature over 37.3, please check your health status very carefully and try answer to symptoms lower

Coughing

If you have dry cough, it could be next symptom. With combine fever could be flu or COVID-19

Tiredness

Specially if you are in most cases tired. COVID-19 could have some light whiles when you feel OK but after few minutes or hours you will feel tiredness again.

Breathing

Be very aware and contact your medical because this could severe case of COVID-19

Prevention

Wash your hands

Wash your hands at least 20 seconds with soap every time when you return from out or if you're touch foreign item.

Cover your face

Please cover your face with mask or scarf to protect yourself and other people. This disease a droplet infection is transmitted. When you're using scarf, please use more layers to better protect.

Social distance

Social distance at least 2m from other persons is best way how can you protect. this is very important indoor but outdoor is very useful too. If you're walking outside and see unknown person walk opposite to you, use other side sidewalk if is it possible.

Sanitize common items

Please, sanitize your cell phone when touch it other members in your house, clean the shower before use. You never know who is infected without symptoms.
